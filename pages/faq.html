Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) 😊

Everything you need to know about participating in our Bonk AirDrop event. If you still have questions, feel free to contact us! We're here to help 🚀

FAQ 🙋‍♂️🙋‍♀️

What is the Bonk AirDrop? 🤔

The Bonk AirDrop is a fantastic opportunity to receive free tokens by completing specific tasks! 🎉 It’s a way to introduce the Bonk token to new users and help grow our amazing community. Join the fun and get rewarded! 💰

How can I participate in the Bonk AirDrop? 📝

It's super easy! Just follow the simple instructions on our website, complete the required steps, and you'll be claiming your tokens in no time! 🎁

Is there a minimum amount I can receive? 💵

Absolutely! The minimum amount you can receive is $50 worth of Bonk tokens. Start earning today! 💸

How do I know if I qualified for the AirDrop? ✅

Once you've completed the steps, we’ll send you a confirmation email! 📧 So, don’t worry — you’ll know right away! 😄

Can I participate if I don't have a wallet? 💼

No problem! 🫶 You’ll need to create a wallet to claim your Bonk tokens. We recommend using MetaMask or Trust Wallet. It's quick and easy! 🔑

How long does the AirDrop last? ⏳

The AirDrop will continue until all tokens are distributed, so hurry up and complete your tasks to make sure you get your share! 🏃‍♂️

Can I refer others to the Bonk AirDrop? 📣

Yes! Share the love by referring your friends and get extra tokens for each successful referral. 🎉 The more, the merrier! 🌟

How can I check my token balance? 💰

Once you receive your tokens, simply open your wallet and voilà! You’ll be able to see your Bonk tokens ready to go! 💳

What do I do if I have not received my tokens? 🚨

If you’ve completed the steps but haven’t received your tokens yet, don’t worry! Reach out to our friendly support team and we’ll get you sorted in no time! 💬

Are the tokens transferable? 🔄

Yes! Once you’ve received your tokens, you’re free to transfer them to other wallets or exchanges. It’s your money! 💸

Still Have Questions? 🤔💬

If you need any further assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out! We’re here to make your experience as smooth as possible! 🌟
